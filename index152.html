According to Colossians 1:15, Jesus is the image of the invisible God, the ______ over all creation.
Ruler
Firstborn
Creator
Master

Correct Answer: Firstborn (Colossians 1:15)

Colossians 1:15 (NIV): "The Son is the image of the invisible God, the firstborn over all creation."

Christ's Supreme Position:

  1. Divine Image: Perfect representation of God
  2. Preeminence: Supreme rank over creation
  3. Authority: Right to rule and inherit
  4. Eternal Nature: Not created but eternal

Context in Colossians 1:15-20:

  • Christ is the visible image of invisible God
  • He existed before anything was created
  • He holds all creation together
  • He is the head of the church
  • God's fullness dwells in Him
  • He reconciled everything through His blood

What "Firstborn" Means:

  • Supreme Rank: Highest position and authority
  • Inheritance Rights: Legal heir of all things
  • Preeminence: First in importance, not time
  • Not Created: Eternal Son, not part of creation
  • Sovereign Rights: Right to rule over all

Biblical Significance of "Firstborn":

  • Old Testament Pattern: Firstborn received double portion and leadership
  • Messianic Title: Points to Christ's supremacy
  • Royal Terminology: Used for kings and rulers
  • Covenant Language: Inheritance and blessing rights

Christ's Relationship to Creation:

  • Agent of Creation: All things created through Him
  • Goal of Creation: All things created for Him
  • Sustainer of Creation: Holds all things together
  • Lord of Creation: Rules over all creation

Theological Implications:

  • Deity of Christ: Equal with God the Father
  • Eternal Sonship: Always existed as Son
  • Cosmic Lordship: Authority over entire universe
  • Mediatorial Role: Bridge between God and creation

Practical Applications for Believers:

  • Worship: Christ deserves our highest praise
  • Submission: Acknowledge His supreme authority
  • Security: Our lives are held together in Him
  • Purpose: We live for His glory and honor
  • Hope: He will bring all things under His rule

Key Insight: As the "firstborn over all creation," Christ holds the supreme position of authority, honor, and inheritance over everything that exists. This title declares His eternal deity, sovereign lordship, and preeminent glory as the perfect image of the invisible God who created, sustains, and rules over all things for His glory.

How many unclean spirits that looked like frogs came out of the mouths of the dragon, beast, and false prophet in Revelation 16:13?
One spirit
Three spirits
Seven spirits
Twelve spirits

Correct Answer: Three spirits - Revelation 16:13

Meaning:

  • Part of the sixth bowl judgment
  • Demon spirits performing deceptive signs
  • Gather kings for the battle of Armageddon
  • Frog-like appearance symbolizes impurity

Key Verse: "And I saw, coming out of the mouth of the dragon and out of the mouth of the beast and out of the mouth of the false prophet, three unclean spirits like frogs." (Revelation 16:13)

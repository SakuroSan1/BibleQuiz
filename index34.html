Correct Answer: Seventh - Jude 1:14

Key Details:

  • Unique biblical reference to Enoch's generational position
  • Connects to Genesis 5 genealogy (Adam → Seth → Enosh → Kenan → Mahalalel → Jared → Enoch)
  • Jude quotes from 1 Enoch 1:9 (non-canonical text)
  • Highlights Enoch's pre-Flood prophetic ministry

Key Verse: "Enoch, the seventh from Adam, prophesied..." (Jude 1:14)

Meaning: The seventh generation signifies: (1) Biblical numerical completeness, (2) God's patience before judgment, and (3) Typological connection to end-times prophecy.

Correct Answer: 12 stars - Revelation 12:1

Key Points from Revelation 12:

  • The woman represents Israel (12 tribes)
  • 12 stars = 12 tribes of Israel
  • The child represents Jesus coming into hearts
  • Cosmic imagery: clothed with sun, moon under feet

Key Verse: "And a great sign appeared in heaven: a woman clothed with the sun, with the moon under her feet, and on her head a crown of twelve stars." (Revelation 12:1)

Connection: Ties to Joseph's dream in Genesis 37:9 where 12 stars represent Jacob's family.

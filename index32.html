BIBLE QUIZ: SEVEN CHURCHES

Revelation 1:10-11
30
The Loud Voice in Revelation 1 says to write this book to ______ churches?
Seven
Twelve
Six
Four

Correct Answer: Seven - Revelation 1:11

The 7 Churches Represent:

  1. Ephesus (Lost first love - Rev 2:1-7)
  2. Smyrna (Persecuted but faithful - Rev 2:8-11)
  3. Pergamum (Compromising church - Rev 2:12-17)
  4. Thyatira (Tolerant of false teaching - Rev 2:18-29)
  5. Sardis (Dead church - Rev 3:1-6)
  6. Philadelphia (Faithful church - Rev 3:7-13)
  7. Laodicea (Lukewarm church - Rev 3:14-22)

Key Verse: "What you see, write in a book and send it to the seven churches: to Ephesus, Smyrna, Pergamum, Thyatira, Sardis, Philadelphia, and Laodicea." (Revelation 1:11)

Meaning: The seven churches represent: (1) Historical churches in Asia Minor, (2) Seven types of spiritual conditions, and (3) Seven church ages throughout history.

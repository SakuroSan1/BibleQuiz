BIBLE QUIZ: TWO TYPES OF CHILDREN

1 John 3:10-15
30
According to 1 John 3, what two types of children are contrasted?
Children of God vs children of devil
Obedient vs disobedient children
Firstborn vs younger children
Spiritual vs carnal children

Correct Answer: Children of God vs children of devil - 1 John 3:10

1. Children of God:

  • Born of God (John 1:12-13)
  • Practice righteousness (1 John 2:29)
  • Love fellow believers (1 John 3:14)
  • Evidence new nature (1 John 3:9)

2. Children of Devil:

  • Display devil's character (John 8:44)
  • Continue in sin (1 John 3:8)
  • Hate reveals true nature (1 John 3:15)
  • Under evil's control (1 John 5:19)

Key Verse: "By this it is evident who are the children of God, and who are the children of the devil: whoever does not practice righteousness is not of God, nor is the one who does not love his brother." (1 John 3:10)

Practical Tests:

  • Righteous living (not sinless perfection)
  • Love for spiritual family
  • Rejection of habitual sin

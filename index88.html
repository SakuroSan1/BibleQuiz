BIBLE QUIZ: MELCHIZEDEK IN HEBREWS 7

Hebrews 7:1-3
30
According to Hebrews 7, who is the one with no records of birth, death, or ancestors?
Moses
Melchizedek
Abraham
Aaron

Correct Answer: Melchizedek (Hebrews 7:3)

Key Points:

  • No genealogy or recorded birth/death (v.3)
  • Priest of "God Most High" (v.1)
  • Received tithes from Abraham (v.2, 4-6)
  • Foreshadowed Christ's eternal priesthood (v.15-17)

Hebrews 7:3: "Without father or mother, without genealogy, without beginning of days or end of life, resembling the Son of God, he remains a priest forever."

Connection to Jesus:

  • Both are eternal priests (v.17, 21)
  • Superior to Levitical priesthood (v.11-14)
  • Jesus is "in the order of Melchizedek" (Psalm 110:4)

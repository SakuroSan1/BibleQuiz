How many 'beasts' are involved with the 'mark of the beast' in Revelation 13?
One beast
Two beasts
Three beasts
Four beasts

Correct Answer: Two beasts - Revelation 13:11-18

Details:

  • First beast from the sea (Rev 13:1-10)
  • Second beast from the earth (Rev 13:11-18)
  • The second beast enforces worship of first beast
  • Mark is associated with the first beast's authority

Key Verses: "Then I saw another beast rising out of the earth... It exercises all the authority of the first beast... and causes the earth to worship the first beast... Also it causes all... to be marked on the right hand or the forehead" (Revelation 13:11-16)

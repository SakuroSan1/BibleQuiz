BIBLE QUIZ: THE ONE MEDIATOR

1 Timothy 2:5
30
According to 1 Timothy 2:5, "For there is one God and one ______ between God and mankind, the man Christ Jesus."
Mediator
Prophet
Messenger
King

Correct Answer: Mediator (1 Timothy 2:5)

1 Timothy 2:5 (NIV): "For there is one God and one mediator between God and mankind, the man Christ Jesus."

The Significance of Christ as Mediator:

  1. Unique Role: Only one mediator between God and humanity
  2. Divine-Human Bridge: Connects holy God with sinful humanity
  3. Fully God, Fully Man: Understands both divine and human perspectives
  4. Redemptive Work: Accomplished through His death and resurrection

What a Mediator Does:

  • Reconciles: Brings together two separated parties
  • Intercedes: Prays and advocates on our behalf
  • Represents: Stands in for both God and humanity
  • Communicates: Reveals God's will to us and presents our needs to God

Biblical Context (1 Timothy 2:1-6):

  • Paul urges prayers for all people
  • God desires all people to be saved
  • Christ gave Himself as a ransom for all
  • The one mediator concept supports universal salvation offer

Practical Applications:

  • Access to God: We can approach God directly through Christ
  • No Other Mediators Needed: Rejects need for human intermediaries
  • Unity in Faith: One way of salvation for all people
  • Assurance: Confidence in Christ's perfect mediation

Key Insight: Christ's unique role as mediator eliminates all other supposed paths to God and establishes the exclusive way of salvation through faith in Him alone.

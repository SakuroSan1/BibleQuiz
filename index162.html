Ephesians 6:16
30
According to Ephesians 6:16, at all times carry faith as your ______ to extinguish all the flaming arrows of the evil one.
Sword
Shield
Armor
Helmet

Correct Answer: Shield (Ephesians 6:16)

Ephesians 6:16 (NIV): "In addition to all this, take up the shield of faith, with which you can extinguish all the flaming arrows of the evil one."

The Shield of Faith:

  1. Defensive Weapon: Protects against spiritual attacks
  2. Active Faith: Must be taken up and used
  3. Complete Protection: Extinguishes ALL flaming arrows
  4. Essential Equipment: Part of God's full armor

Context in Ephesians 6:10-18:

  • Paul's description of the full armor of God
  • Spiritual battle against evil forces
  • Six pieces of spiritual armor
  • Shield of faith is the fourth piece mentioned
  • Emphasis on standing firm against devil's schemes

Roman Military Context:

  • Scutum: Large rectangular shield used by Roman soldiers
  • Flaming Arrows: Arrows dipped in pitch and set on fire
  • Protection: Shield could extinguish arrows and provide cover
  • Turtle Formation: Soldiers interlocked shields for maximum protection

What "Flaming Arrows" Represent:

  • Doubts: Attacks on God's character and promises
  • Temptations: Enticements to sin and compromise
  • Fear: Anxiety about future and circumstances
  • Discouragement: Feelings of failure and hopelessness
  • Accusations: Condemnation about past sins

How Faith Functions as a Shield:

  • Trust in God: Confidence in His character and promises
  • Biblical Truth: Faith based on God's Word
  • Active Defense: Must be consciously used
  • Complete Coverage: Protects entire person from attacks
  • Community Aspect: Can protect others when used collectively

Practical Applications:

  • Daily Preparation: Consciously take up faith each day
  • Scripture Memory: God's promises as basis for faith
  • Prayer: Activating faith through communication with God
  • Worship: Focusing on God's character builds faith
  • Community: Strengthening faith through fellowship

The Six Pieces of Armor:

  • Belt of Truth: God's truth as foundation
  • Breastplate of Righteousness: Christ's righteousness protecting heart
  • Gospel of Peace: Readiness to share and stand on gospel
  • Shield of Faith: Protection against attacks
  • Helmet of Salvation: Assurance protecting mind
  • Sword of the Spirit: God's Word as offensive weapon

Modern Spiritual Warfare:

  • Cultural Pressures: Faith against secular ideologies
  • Personal Struggles: Faith in God's goodness amid suffering
  • Relational Conflicts: Faith for forgiveness and reconciliation
  • Ministry Challenges: Faith for gospel effectiveness
  • Internal Battles: Faith against sinful desires and thoughts

Key Insight: The shield of faith is not passive belief but active trust in God that we must deliberately take up and use to defend against the devil's multifaceted attacks. When wielded effectively, it doesn't just block but completely extinguishes every fiery assault, leaving the enemy powerless against our confident trust in God's character, promises, and power.

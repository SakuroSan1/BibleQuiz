Daily Bible Quiz

According to 1 Timothy 4, in later times some will abandon the faith and follow deceiving spirits and teachings of...?
1 Timothy 4:1
Time left: 30 seconds
  • Angels
  • Demons
  • Humans
  • Prophets

Explanation: 1 Timothy 4:1 says, "The Spirit clearly says that in later times some will abandon the faith and follow deceiving spirits and things taught by demons."

Key Points from 1 Timothy 4:1-5:

  • Source: This warning comes directly from the Holy Spirit
  • Timing: Refers to "later times" (end times)
  • Action: People will abandon the true faith
  • Deception: Follow deceiving spirits and demonic teachings
  • Examples: False teachers will forbid marriage and certain foods
  • Truth: Everything God created is good and should be received with thanksgiving

Spiritual Significance: This verse warns believers to be discerning about teachings that claim to be spiritual but actually originate from demonic sources rather than from God's Word.

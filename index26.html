How many wings did each of the 4 living creatures have in Revelation 4?
4 wings
6 wings
8 wings
12 wings

Correct Answer: 6 wings - Revelation 4:8

Key Details:

  • Four living creatures around God's throne (Rev 4:6)
  • Each had six wings (Rev 4:8)
  • Covered with eyes, representing God's omniscience
  • Similar to Isaiah's seraphim (Isaiah 6:2)
  • Continuously worship day and night

Key Verse: "Each of the four living creatures had six wings and was covered with eyes all around, even under its wings. Day and night they never stop saying: 'Holy, holy, holy is the Lord God Almighty,' who was, and is, and is to come." (Revelation 4:8)

Meaning: The six wings may symbolize: (1) Readiness for God's service, (2) Complete spiritual mobility, (3) Connection to heavenly worship patterns, and (4) Protection of God's holiness.

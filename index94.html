BIBLE QUIZ: POWER OF GOD'S WORD

Hebrews 4:12
30
According to Hebrews 4:12, the word of God is sharper than a ____-edged sword.
Single
Double
Dull
Golden

Correct Answer: Double (Hebrews 4:12)

Hebrews 4:12 (NIV): "For the word of God is alive and active. Sharper than any double-edged sword, it penetrates even to dividing soul and spirit, joints and marrow; it judges the thoughts and attitudes of the heart."

Key Characteristics of God's Word:

  • Alive: Has life-giving power (John 6:63)
  • Active: Accomplishes God's purposes (Isaiah 55:11)
  • Sharp: Cuts through deception and pretense
  • Penetrating: Reaches deepest parts of our being
  • Judging: Reveals true motives and thoughts

Why "Double-edged"?

  • Cuts both ways - convicts and comforts
  • Defends and attacks spiritual forces
  • Works internally and externally
  • Historical and contemporary relevance

Practical Application:

  • Allows God's Word to examine your heart
  • Use it as your spiritual weapon (Ephesians 6:17)
  • Let it transform your thinking (Romans 12:2)

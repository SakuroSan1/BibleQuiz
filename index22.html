How many differently colored horses are mentioned in Revelation 6?
3 horses
4 horses
5 horses
7 horses

Correct Answer: 4 horses - Revelation 6:1-8

The Four Horses:

  1. White horse (conquest) - Rev 6:2
  2. Red horse (war) - Rev 6:4
  3. Black horse (famine) - Rev 6:5-6
  4. Pale horse (death) - Rev 6:8

Key Verse: "I looked, and there before me was a pale horse! Its rider was named Death, and Hades was following close behind him." (Revelation 6:8)

Significance: The four horses represent sequential end-time judgments showing God's sovereign control over human history, with each color symbolizing a different aspect of divine wrath.

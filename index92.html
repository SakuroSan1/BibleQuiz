BIBLE QUIZ: SPIRITUAL MATURITY

Hebrews 5:12-14
30
According to Hebrews 5, the mature ones need God's Word in:
Milk form
Solid food form
Parables only
Written letters only

Correct Answer: Solid food form (Hebrews 5:14)

Key Contrasts:

Milk (v.12-13) Solid Food (v.14)
For spiritual infants For the mature
Elementary truths Deeper doctrine
Basic teachings Discernment training

Characteristics of the Mature:

  • Trained by constant practice (v.14)
  • Can distinguish good from evil (v.14)
  • Teachers (should be), not perpetual students (v.12)

Warning: Stagnation is dangerous (Hebrews 6:1-3)

Key Verse: "Solid food is for the mature, who by constant use have trained themselves to distinguish good from evil." (Hebrews 5:14)

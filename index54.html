According to 2 Peter 1:9, what describes someone who lacks these spiritual qualities?
Blind and shortsighted
Physically weak
Poor and hungry
Lonely and depressed
Key Verse: "But he that lacketh these things is blind, and cannot see afar off, and hath forgotten that he was purged from his old sins." (2 Peter 1:9 KJV)
Key Points:
  • Refers to the qualities listed in 2 Peter 1:5-7 (faith, virtue, knowledge, etc.)
  • Spiritual blindness results from neglecting spiritual growth
  • Forgetting God's forgiveness leads to shortsightedness

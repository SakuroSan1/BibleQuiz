BIBLE QUIZ: HANDLING FALSE DOCTRINE

2 John 1:10-11
30
In 2 John, what are the 2 things said to do when anyone comes to us with false doctrines?
Receive & Welcome
Reject & Don't Welcome
Debate & Tolerate
Ignore & Avoid

Correct Answer: Reject & Don't Welcome - 2 John 1:10-11

Key Verse: "If anyone comes to you and does not bring this teaching, do not receive him into your house or give him any greeting, for whoever greets him takes part in his wicked works." (2 John 1:10-11)

Detailed Explanation:

  1. Reject False Teachers:
    • Do not receive into your home (v.10)
    • Refuse to endorse their ministry
    • Protect the church from deception
  2. Don't Welcome Them:
    • Do not give greeting (v.11)
    • Avoid formal recognition
    • Prevent sharing in their evil works

Context:

  • Warning against deceivers (v.7)
  • Those who deny Christ's incarnation
  • Persistent false teachers (not seekers with questions)

Balance:

  • Love must be according to truth (v.1-3)
  • Different from correcting believers (Gal 6:1)
  • Not contradicting evangelism (Matt 28:19)

According to 2 Timothy 1:7, the Spirit God gives us power, love, and ______?
Wealth
Self-discipline
Knowledge
Success

Correct Answer: Self-discipline (2 Timothy 1:7)

2 Timothy 1:7 (NIV): "For the Spirit God gave us does not make us timid, but gives us power, love and self-discipline."

The Threefold Gift of the Spirit:

  1. Power: Courage and strength to face challenges
  2. Love: God's agape love for others
  3. Self-discipline: Sound mind and self-control

What This Means for Believers:

  • Not Fear: The Spirit doesn't make us timid or fearful
  • Divine Enablement: These are supernatural gifts, not natural abilities
  • Balanced Christian Life: Power + Love + Discipline = Spiritual maturity
  • For Ministry: Essential qualities for effective service

Practical Applications:

  • Power: Boldness in witness and overcoming obstacles
  • Love: Compassionate service and forgiveness
  • Self-discipline: Consistency in prayer, Bible study, and holy living
  • Together: Prevents abuse of spiritual gifts

Biblical Context: Paul wrote this to encourage Timothy who was facing ministry challenges and needed reassurance of God's enabling Spirit.

Key Insight: The Holy Spirit provides everything we need to fulfill God's calling without fear or compromise.

Daily Bible Quiz

According to 1 Timothy 6, "For the love of ______ is a root of all kinds of evil."
1 Timothy 6:10
Time left: 30 seconds
  • Power
  • Money
  • Pleasure
  • Praise

Explanation: 1 Timothy 6:10 says, "For the love of money is a root of all kinds of evil. Some people, eager for money, have wandered from the faith and pierced themselves with many griefs."

This verse warns against making wealth the primary focus of life, as it can lead to temptation and spiritual downfall.

How many thousands from each of the 12 tribes of Israel make up the 144,000 sealed servants in Revelation 7?
10 thousand
12 thousand
14 thousand
20 thousand

Correct Answer: 12 thousand - Revelation 7:4-8

Key Details:

  • 12 tribes × 12,000 = 144,000 (Rev 7:4-8)
  • Sealed before trumpet judgments (Rev 7:3)
  • Protected during tribulation
  • Symbolic of complete divine preservation

Key Verse: "Then I heard the number of those who were sealed: 144,000 from all the tribes of Israel... 12,000 from each tribe." (Revelation 7:4-8)

Significance: The 12,000 from each tribe represents: (1) Perfect divine numbering (12×12×1000), (2) Equal representation of all Israel, (3) Contrast to the innumerable multitude in Rev 7:9, and (4) Fulfillment of Ezekiel 9's sealing vision.

BIBLE QUIZ: 3 JOHN CONTRASTS

3 John 1:1-14
30
How many members from the church are mainly contrasted (other than the letter holder) in 3 John?
Two
Three
One
Four

Correct Answer: Two - Gaius vs Diotrephes (3 John 1:9-10)

Detailed Contrast in 3 John:

  1. Gaius (3 John 1:1-8):
    • Faithful church member
    • Known for hospitality to traveling ministers
    • "Walk in truth" (v.3-4)
    • Supports missionaries financially (v.5-8)
  2. Diotrephes (3 John 1:9-11):
    • Power-hungry leader
    • Rejects apostolic authority ("loves to be first")
    • Spreads malicious words
    • Refuses hospitality and excommunicates believers

Demetrius (3 John 1:12):

  • Third mentioned but not contrasted
  • Holds good testimony from all
  • Possible letter carrier
  • Example of good reputation

Key Lessons:

  • Early church faced leadership conflicts
  • God honors faithful servants like Gaius
  • Warns against domineering leaders like Diotrephes
  • Demetrius models Christian integrity

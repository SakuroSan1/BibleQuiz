BIBLE QUIZ: MINISTERING ANGELS

Hebrews 1:14
30
According to Hebrews 1:14, angels are considered as ______ spirits.
Holy
Ministering
Guardian
Heavenly

Correct Answer: Ministering (Hebrews 1:14)

Hebrews 1:14 (ESV): "Are they not all ministering spirits sent out to serve for the sake of those who are to inherit salvation?"

Key Truths About Angels:

  • Servants, Not Objects of Worship: Angels are created beings who serve God (Colossians 2:18)
  • Purpose: Sent to serve those who will inherit salvation
  • Christ's Superiority: Hebrews 1 shows Jesus is far above angels (v.4-13)
  • Not Independent: They operate under God's authority and command

Biblical Examples of Angelic Ministry:

  • Protected Daniel in the lions' den (Daniel 6:22)
  • Strengthened Jesus in Gethsemane (Luke 22:43)
  • Released apostles from prison (Acts 5:19)
  • Guided Philip to the Ethiopian eunuch (Acts 8:26)
  • Announced Jesus' birth to shepherds (Luke 2:9-14)

Important Balance:

  • Angels are real and active in God's plan
  • But they are not to be worshipped or prayed to (Revelation 22:8-9)
  • Our focus should remain on Christ, who is superior to angels
  • They are God's messengers and servants, not independent agents

Practical Application: Take comfort that God uses His angels to minister to His children, but keep your worship and focus on Christ alone.

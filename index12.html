After how many days did God make the two prophets become alive again in Revelation 11?
1 day
2.5 days
3.5 days
7 days

Correct Answer: 3.5 days - Revelation 11:11

Key Details:

  • Two witnesses prophesy for 1,260 days (Rev 11:3)
  • Killed by the beast from the Abyss (Rev 11:7)
  • Bodies lie in Jerusalem for 3.5 days (Rev 11:8-9)
  • Resurrected after 3.5 days (Rev 11:11)
  • Symbolic of Christ's resurrection power

Key Verse: "But after three and a half days, the breath of life from God entered them, and they stood on their feet, and great fear fell on those who saw them." (Revelation 11:11)
